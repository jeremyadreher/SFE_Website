SFE at the 2018 Farnborough International Airshow
 
   
Silicon Forest Electronics will return as an exhibitor at the 2018 Farnborough International Airshow, June 16-22, as part of the Washington State Pavilion.  Held every other year outside London, the Farnborough International Airshow is one of the largest of its kind, drawing hundreds of defense and commercial aerospace firms and hundreds of thousands of visitors. 

For more information, go to Farnborough Air Show or to connect at the Airshow, please contact Frank Nichols ( frank@si-forest.com).
 
SFE at the 2018 Energy Drone Coalition Summit
 
 
 
Silicon Forest Electronics will participate as an exhibitor at the 2018 Energy Drone Coalition Summit, held June 20-21 in Houston, Texas.    The Summit is an event exclusively focused on the business and technology of Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAV's) and robotics (aerial, ground/surface & subsea) in energy operations. 

For more information go to Energy Drone Coalition or contact Frank Nichols ( frank@si-forest.com).
