Heli-Expo 2018 Ahead!

   
Silicon Forest Electronics will return as an exhibitor to the Heli-Expo, to be held, Feb. 27- Mar. 1, in the Las Vegas Convention Center.  The Heli-Expo is the world's largest trade show dedicated to the international helicopter community.  
 
As a Helicopter Association International member and key provider of electronics manufacturing services to the aviation, aerospace & unmanned systems industries, SFE will showcase unique solutions and capabilities to developers and integrators of rotorcraft systems.
