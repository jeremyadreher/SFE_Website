Employee Spotlight - Welcome Kami Tomlinson!
 
 
Kami Tomlinson has joined the SFE team in the role of Human Resources Specialist. Kami enjoys working with and supporting employees in every stage of their careers; from recruiting, through career development, to retirement. She recently moved from Tillamook, Oregon having worked in Human Resources for over 12 years at an aerospace manufacturing and unmanned aerial systems flight testing company.  On the weekends, she likes exploring new places in Vancouver with her family.  You might see her outside of the office at the archery range shooting her Olympic recurve bow, flinging arrows downrange!
 
Kami's favorite quote is ""We do the difficult immediately; the impossible takes a little longer." (US Army Corps of Engineers - WWII)
 
We are excited to have Kami on the Silicon Forest Electronics team!
