About Medical Teams International

Thank you for joining us in our commitment to rise, mobilize and make a difference. Your support of Medical Teams International (MTI) will help save and improve lives worldwide. Last year, MTI provided lifesaving care and humanitarian aid to 2.7 million people in 70 countries around the world. Their mobile dental program provided urgently-needed dental care to 17,000 in the Pacific Northwest. MTI adheres to the highest standards of stewardship and accountibility. More than 97% of donated resources we receive go to help people in need. For four consecutive years, Charity Navigator has awarded MTI four stars, its highest rating. For more information, please visit www.medicalteams.org
